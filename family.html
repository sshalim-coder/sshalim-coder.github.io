💌 Family Words for When You Feel Alone

Not everyone has a family to turn to. This page is for those moments when you just need a loving word — like from a mom, a dad, or a sibling.

🧕 From a Mom:

"Baby, I’m proud of you. I know life gets heavy, but look at you — you’re still here. Make sure you eat, okay? You need your strength. You are never too much, and you are always enough."

👨‍🦳 From a Dad:

"You don’t have to have it all figured out. You’re doing better than you think. Keep your head up — I believe in you. And I always will."

👧 From a Big Sister:

"Hey, don’t be so hard on yourself. Cry it out if you need to. Then fix your crown and keep going. I’m cheering for you even on your quiet days."
